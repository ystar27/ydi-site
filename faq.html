FAQ

Frequently asked question

WHAT KIND OF ORGANISATION IS YOUNGSTARS FOUNDATION?

Youngstars Foundation is an independent NGO (non-profit) registered in Nigeria and Ghana.

WHO STARTED YOUNGSTARS FOUNDATION AND WHY?

Youngstars Foundation was started by Kingsely Bangwell from a weekly youth club in a local barber shop in 1995. Youngstars Foundation is growing to emerge as one of the lead youth non-profit that is building young people and strengthening youth organizations involved in development programs in Africa.

WHERE IS YOUNGSTARS FOUNDATION HEAD OFFICE LOCATED?

Youngstars Foundation is one of Africa’s unique, life-changing youth organization operating in Abuja, Nigeria at No 1 Waziri Ibrahim Cresent, Off Ahmadu Bello Way, Gudu Abuja, Nigeria.

IS YOUNGSTARS FOUNDATION SUPPORTED BY DONORS ?

Yes, We are Supported by Humanitarian Donors.

WHAT ARE THE BENEFITS OF JOINING YOUNGSTARS FOUNDATION COMMUNITIES?

Youngstars Foundation is increasing the quantity of high pedigree young leaders to achieve global impact, engaging in capacity building platforms for youth to actively participate in promoting public accountability, impactful and sustainable democratic governance, raising youth voice and awareness to actively participate in public leadership at all levels and to actualize youth priorities and Boosting youth collaboration towards homegrown African innovations, inventions and solutions for the continents challenges.

HOW CAN I JOIN YOUNGSTARS AS AN INDIVIDUAL OR AN ORGANIZATION?

As a Volunteer, as a Participant, as a Freelancer, as a Partner or an Organization call (+234) 703 553 5876 for more details

WHAT ARE THE FOCUS AREAS OF YOUNGSTARS FOUNDATION?

Democracy, Leadership, Innovation and Gender

IS YOUNGSTARS FOUNDATION PARTISAN / BELONG TO ANY POLITICAL PARTY?

No, Youngstars Foundation is Non Partisan and would not identify with any political party in any form.

WHAT IS THE IMPACT OF WORK CARRIED OUT BY YOUNGSTARS FOUNDATION?

Youngstars Foundation is contributing to the creation of an Africa where the youth are deliberately developed to reach their fullest potentials, and actively leading the continent to sustainable peace, good governance, development and prosperit

WHICH DONORS CURRENTLY FUND YOUNGSTARS FOUNDATION?

Youngstars Foundation is Currently Funded by Humanitarian donors like National Endowment for Democracy(NED), National Democratic Institute(NDI), United Nations Women, Ford Foundation.

Youngstars Foundation is an African Youth NGO that is raising young people and building youth organizations to proactively participate in improving governance, gender equality, access to transformative education and quality leadership practice in the continent.
