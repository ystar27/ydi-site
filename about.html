About us

Youngstars Foundation

Starting in a local barber shop in Jos Plateau state in 1995, Youngstars has grown into a Pan African youth focused NGO. Since inception, Youngstars has conducted about 60 youth workshops directly and facilitated over 500 step down youth activities within and outside Nigeria. It has also produced & aired over two hundred television/ radio programs and produced several film documentaries including "My Vote Fit Change Nigeria" which US Secretary of State, Hillary Clinton tweeted about in 2011.

Our mission

Youngstars Foundation is an African Youth NGO that is raising young people and building youth organizations to proactively participate in improving governance, gender equality, access to transformative education and quality leadership practice in the continent.

Our vision

A developed Africa where her youth and females reach their fullest potentials, and are actively leading the continent to sustainable peace, good governance, development and inclusive prosperity.

Our Objectives

1. Building youth capacity and resources to actively participate in impactful governance, development and politics.

2. Promoting gender equality, women’s advancement and voice.

3. Providing access to transformative low-cost education to children from low-income and poorest households.

4. Inculcating transformational leadership practice among young people.

Our Theory of Change

We believe that great citizens build great nations. We also believe that every young person, including females, have great potentials and a right to fully develop themselves to productively contribute to national development and prosperity.

A society that inadequately prepares her youth resource and discriminates against females reduces their potentials for greatness and misses out on the unquantifiable contribution’s quality citizens bring to national development. Strategically equipping the youth with transformational values, quality knowledge, problem solving skills and an inspirational future of possibilities raises their personal aspirations, quality and civic responsiveness.

Most importantly, social equality and inclusion increases the chances of every citizen to aspire high and endeavour to create transformational experiences in private or public spaces that will foster human dignity, just societies, freedom and national development.
